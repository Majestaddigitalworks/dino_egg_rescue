Dino Egg Rescue

Save the eggs, hatch the babies, build your dino family.

Saved: 0
Babies: 0
Lives: 3
Move: Arrow Keys or WASD Goal: Touch eggs to rescue them. Every 3 eggs = 1 baby dino joins you.

Story idea: Big Dino saves the eggs before the rocks hit them. When enough eggs are rescued, a baby dino hatches and starts following Big Dino.
